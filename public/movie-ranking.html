My Top 3 Movies of All-Time.

The Dark Knight

This movie continues to be one of the greatest superhero stories to date.
The realism of the character arcs and the deeper meaning land this title at the top of my list

Interstellar

Interstellar is a movie that has a special place in my heart.
The story of love and sacrifice is beautifully told and the visuals are stunning.

The plot, cinema, and all other aspects of this film blew me away in theatres
